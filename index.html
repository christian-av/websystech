Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken or pork with soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, cook the marinated meat over medium heat until the meat is browned.
  3. Add water if needed and simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Adjust the taste by adding more soy sauce or vinegar according to your preference.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious adobo!
